Supporters

Creative Aging welcomes the financial support from individuals, companies and foundations as an important way to help fulfill our mission to serve as many seniors as possible with professional arts and humanities programs that are delivered in nursing homes, assisted living facilities, senior centers and day programs across the region.

If you have any questions or would like to designate a gift to honor someone or sponsor a facility, please call Bev Ross, Executive Director at 513-561-7500.

To give a tax deductible donation, please click on the donate button in the left hand collumn of the webpage or send your check to:
Creative Aging Cincinnati
P.O. Box 428638
Cininnati, Ohio 45242
