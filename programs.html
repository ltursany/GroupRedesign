In-Facility Programs

Have elders who cannot travel? No worries.
Enjoy our variety of programs to be hosted right
in your facility!


Holiday Season Programs

Featuring a trombone ensemble by Bones of Cincinnatus, Brian and Susan Deyo with guitar and vocals, and many more all in time for the winter holidays


Drama and other Unique Programs

Anything from engaging presentations from Cincinnati Zoo volunteers to Dramakinetics: an educational workshop on the therapeutic benefits of drama


Art Programs

Art classes led by artist Cheryl Pannabecker, expressive therapy led by Rachel Demir, and speakers from the Cincinnati Art Museum


History and Culture Programs

Offers programs from the Cincinnati Preservation Association, Janice ("Sunflower") Trytten on Native American history and culture, and Rita Wetterstroem with reading programs to help elders rekindle their memories


Music Programs

NEW programs and performances from Rob Lowe, Desafinado, Les Tacy, and the barbershop quartet Kaleidoscope!


Wellness Programs

Multiple active programs in yoga, tai chi, dance, mountain dulcimer, and recreational group drumming to fit every elder's needs


Vern and Aileen Kohler Dementia Program

Provides programming to individuals that reside in a facility and are unable to attend group programs due to cognitive and physical challenges
